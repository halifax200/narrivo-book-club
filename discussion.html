The Midnight Library

Matt Haig

Discussion Guide: January Pick

Question 1

Nora Seed experiences "the library between life and death." If you had your own library of regrets, which "volume" would you be most tempted to open first?

Question 2

The Librarian, Mrs. Elm, says, "You don't have to understand life. You just have to live it." Do you agree with this philosophy, or is understanding necessary for peace?

Question 3

How did the concept of the "Root Life" affect your perspective on your own current path?

Leader's Tip:

Encourage members to share one "small regret" they've let go of during this reading session to build intimacy in the group.
